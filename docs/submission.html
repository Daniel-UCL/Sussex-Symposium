Submission Information

Initial submission type: Extended abstract for review (600 - max 1000 words)
plus a short abstract for the program and proceedings (max 300 words)

Submission guidance

Important Dates:

  • Submission deadline: 28 February 2026
  • Notification of acceptance: 31 March 2026
  • Camera-ready paper submission deadline: 28 April 2026

Camera-ready paper length: Maximum of 8 pages, including notes and references.

Presentation format: In-person oral presentations only (20 minutes + max 10 minutes for questions). One full-day symposium. We may run a separate track for student presentations.

Proceedings: Full papers accepted for the symposium will normally be published as part of the AISB 2026 Proceedings. Completed camera-ready copy (using the AISB format) should be sent by 28 April 2026. Papers not sent by that date may not be included.
